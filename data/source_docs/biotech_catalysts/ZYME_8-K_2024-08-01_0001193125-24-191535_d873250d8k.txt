Item 8.01

Other Events.

On August 1, 2024, Zymeworks Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that its Board of Directors had authorized a Share Repurchase Program under which the Company may repurchase up to $60,000,000 of the Company’s common stock, par value $0.00001 per share. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.

 

Item 7.01

Regulation FD Disclosure.

Assuming the full execution of the Share Repurchase Program, the Company expects its existing cash resources as of June 30, 2024, when combined with certain anticipated regulatory milestone payments, will enable it to fund planned operations into the second half of 2027.
