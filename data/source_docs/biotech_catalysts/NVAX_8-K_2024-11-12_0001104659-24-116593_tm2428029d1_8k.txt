Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On
October 16, 2024, Novavax disclosed that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (“FDA”) had placed a clinical hold on
Novavax’s Investigational New Drug Application (“IND”) for its COVID-19-Influenza Combination and stand-alone
influenza vaccine candidates. On November 11, 2024, Novavax announced that the FDA has removed the previously disclosed clinical
hold on Novavax’s IND for its COVID-19-Influenza Combination and stand-alone influenza vaccine candidates.
