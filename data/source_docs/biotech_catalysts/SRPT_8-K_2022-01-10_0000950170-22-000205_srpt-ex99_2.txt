Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) DMD affects approximately 1 in 3,500-5,000 males worldwide1 DMD is a rare, fatal neuromuscular genetic disease inherited in an X-linked recessive pattern2 Muscle weakness becomes increasingly noticeable by 3 to 5 years of age, and most patients use a wheelchair by the time they are 11 years old2 During adolescence, cardiac and respiratory muscle deterioration lead to serious, life-threatening complications3 1.
