Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On July 13, 2023, Iovance
Biotherapeutics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the closing of the sale of an aggregate of 23,000,000
shares of its common stock, $0.000041666 par value per share, in its previously disclosed public offering, including 3,000,000 shares
issued pursuant to the exercise of the option granted to the underwriters, at a public offering price of $7.50 per share before underwriting
discounts and commissions. The total net proceeds to the Company from the offering, including the exercise of the option by the underwriters,
are expected to be approximately $161.4 million after deducting the underwriting discounts and commissions and estimated offering expenses
payable by the Company.
