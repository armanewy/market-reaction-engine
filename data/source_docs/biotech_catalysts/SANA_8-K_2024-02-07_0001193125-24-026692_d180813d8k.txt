Item 8.01 Other Events.

Preliminary Financial Information (Unaudited)

Sana Biotechnology, Inc. (the “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) reports that it estimates its cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities as of January 2, 2024 were approximately $205.4 million. This estimate of the Company’s cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities as of January 2, 2024 is preliminary, has not been audited, is based on information available to the Company as of the filing of this Current Report on Form 8-K (this “Report”), and is subject to change. Additional information and disclosure would be required for a more complete understanding of the Company’s financial position as of January 2, 2024. The Company’s independent registered public accounting firm has not audited, reviewed or performed any procedures with respect to this preliminary information and, accordingly, does not express an opinion or any other form of assurance about it.
