Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On October 2, 2023, Rocket Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the “Company”) announced that the U.S.
Food and Drug Administration has accepted the Biologics License Application and granted Priority Review for KRESLADI (RP-L201; marnetegragene autotemcel), a lentiviral vector-based investigational gene
therapy for severe Leukocyte Adhesion Deficiency-1. A copy of the press release issued in connection with the announcement is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on
Form 8-K and is incorporated into this report by reference.
