Item 7.01.

Regulation FD Disclosure.

On November 12, 2021, Robert L. Avery, M.D., Founder of California Retina Consultants and Research Foundation, and a principal investigator for REGENXBIO Inc.’s (the “Company”) Phase II AAVIATE and ALTITUDE clinical trials utilizing in-office suprachoroidal delivery of RGX-314 for the treatment of wet age-related macular degeneration and diabetic retinopathy, respectively, presented interim analyses of data from the AAVIATE and ALTITUDE trials at the American Academy of Ophthalmology 2021 Annual Meeting. A copy of Dr. Avery’s presentation materials is available on the “Presentations and Publications” section of the Company’s website at www.regenxbio.com.

The information in Item 7.01 of this Current Report on Form 8-K and the presentation materials on the Company’s website shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to liability under that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.

 

Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On November 12, 2021, the Company issued a press release announcing interim analyses of data from the AAVIATE and ALTITUDE trials. A copy of the press release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
