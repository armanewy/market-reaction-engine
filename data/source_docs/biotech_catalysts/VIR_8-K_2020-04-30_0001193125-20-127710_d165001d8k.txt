Item 8.01

Other Events.

On April 29, 2020, Vir Biotechnology, Inc. (the “Company”) closed the previously announced sale of 6,626,027 shares of the Company’s common
stock (the “Shares”) to Glaxo Group Limited (“GGL”), an affiliate of GlaxoSmithKline Intellectual Property Development Limited and GlaxoSmithKline Biologicals SA, at a price per Share of $37.73, for an aggregate purchase price of
approximately $250.0 million. The closing was subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions, including the expiration or termination of the applicable waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976,
as amended, which early termination was effective on April 22, 2020. The Shares were issued pursuant to a stock purchase agreement between the Company and GGL dated April 5, 2020, as previously disclosed in the Company’s Current
Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 6, 2020.
