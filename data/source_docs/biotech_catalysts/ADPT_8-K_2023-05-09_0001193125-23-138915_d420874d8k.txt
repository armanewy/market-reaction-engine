Item 7.01.

Regulation FD Disclosure

On December 19, 2018, Adaptive Biotechnologies Corporation (the “Company”) entered into a Strategic Collaboration and License Agreement (the “Agreement”) with Genentech, Inc. (“Genentech”) to research, develop and commercialize neoantigen-directed T-cell therapies for cancer (each, a “Therapy”). On May 9, 2023, Genentech advised the Company that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration has accepted an investigational new drug (IND) application submitted for use of the Therapy.
