On May 9, 2024, the Company reported topline safety and tolerability data from
its Phase 2 study of treprostinil palmitil inhalation powder (“TPIP”) in patients with pulmonary hypertension associated with interstitial lung disease and provided an update on blinded data from its ongoing Phase 2 study of TPIP in pulmonary arterial hypertension.
