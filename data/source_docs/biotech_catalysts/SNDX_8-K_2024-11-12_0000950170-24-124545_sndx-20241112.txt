Item 8.01 Other Events.

On November 12, 2024, Syndax Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing positive topline results from the relapsed or refractory mutant NPM1 (“mNPM1”) acute myeloid leukemia cohort in the pivotal Phase 2 portion of the AUGMENT-101 trial of revumenib, an oral, small molecule menin inhibitor. A copy of the press release is filed herewith as Exhibit 99.1. The Company is holding a conference call regarding the announcement on November 12, 2024. The information contained in the press release is incorporated by reference into this Current Report on Form 8-K.
