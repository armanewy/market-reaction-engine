Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On March 26, 2021, Rocket Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a notice of full redemption in respect of its 6.25% Convertible Senior Notes due
2022 (the “2022 Notes”), approximately $38.35 million principal amount of which was outstanding as of such date. Prior to April 26, 2021, holders of approximately $38.35 million principal amount of the Notes converted such Notes in accordance with
the terms of the governing indenture into approximately 1.3 million shares of the Company’s common stock and cash in lieu of fractional shares.
