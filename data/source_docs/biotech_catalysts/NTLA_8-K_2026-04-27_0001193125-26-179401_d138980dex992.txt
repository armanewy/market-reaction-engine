About HAE and Lonvo-z Dr. David Lebwohl Chief Medical Officer, Intellia Therapeutics

Hereditary Angioedema (HAE): Currently a Lifelong Condition with Significant Burden Rare, genetic and
life-threatening disease • Caused by a hereditary deficiency or dysfunction of the C1 inhibitor protein that leads to an imbalance in the kallikrein-kinin system and an overproduction of bradykinin • Patients experience unpredictable,
recurrent, painful and potentially life-threatening swelling attacks1,2 • Symptoms often begin in the first decade of life and typically worsen in puberty3,4 • Attacks can be triggered by stress, trauma, infection, fatigue and hormones2
