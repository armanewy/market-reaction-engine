Item 8.01 Other Events.

On March 10, 2026, CRISPR Therapeutics AG (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that it had commenced a private offering, subject to market conditions and other factors, of $350.0 million aggregate principal amount of convertible senior notes due 2031 (the “notes”) to persons reasonably believed to be qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.

On March 11, 2026, the Company issued a press release announcing the pricing of an upsized offering of the notes, increasing the size of the offering to an aggregate principal amount of $550 million.

Copies of the press releases announcing (1) the commencement of the private offering of the notes and (2) the pricing of the notes, respectively, are filed as Exhibit 99.1 and Exhibit 99.2 hereto and are incorporated herein by reference.

Neither this Current Report on Form 8-K nor the press releases constitute an offer to sell, or the solicitation of an offer to buy, the notes or the Company’s common shares, if any, issuable upon conversion of the notes.
