Item 8.01

Other Events.

On January 26, 2023, Sana Biotechnology, Inc. (the “Company”) announced that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration has cleared the Company’s Investigational New Drug (IND) application to initiate a first-in-human study of SC291, a CD19-targeted allogeneic CAR T cell therapy that was developed using the Company’s hypoimmune platform, in patients with various B-cell malignancies. A copy of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
