Item 7.01

Regulation FD Disclosure.

On October 13, 2022, Kymera Therapeutics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing its plans to hold an investor webcast on December 14, 2022 to share clinical data from its KT-474 Phase I patient cohort and oncology pipeline, the completion of dosing in its KT-474 Phase 1 HS and AD patient cohort (Part C), and updates on its KT-413 and KT-333 and KT-253 programs.
