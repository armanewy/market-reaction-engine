Item 8.01

Other Events.

On January 26, 2021, Apellis Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the “Company”) closed its previously announced privately negotiated exchange (the “Exchange Transactions”) with certain holders of its 3.500% Convertible Senior Notes due 2026 (the “Notes”) issued on September 16, 2019.

In the Exchange Transactions, the holders exchanged approximately $126.1 million in aggregate principal amount of Notes and the Company issued an aggregate of 3,906,869 shares of its common stock.
