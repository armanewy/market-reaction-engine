Item 8.01 Other Events.

On November 5, 2024, Beam Therapeutics Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the release of abstracts accepted for presentation at the American Society of Hematology Annual Meeting taking place in San Diego, CA from December 7-10, 2024, including two abstracts on initial data from the Company's BEACON clinical trial of BEAM-101 for the treatment of sickle cell disease, one on initial data for the Company's Phase 1/2 clinical trial of BEAM-201 for the treatment of relapsed, refractory T-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia/T cell lymphoblastic lymphoma, and one on preclinical non-human primate data for the Company's Engineered Stem Cell Antibody Paired Evasion conditioning platform. A copy of the Company’s press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference.
