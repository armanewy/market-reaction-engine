Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On October 14, 2025, Rocket Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the “Company”) announced that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (the “FDA”) has accepted resubmission
of the Biologics License Application (BLA) for KRESLADI™ (marnetegragene autotemcel; marne-cel), a lentiviral vector (LV)-based investigational gene therapy for severe Leukocyte Adhesion Deficiency-I (LAD-I), a rare genetic immune disorder that
predisposes patients to recurrent and fatal infections and is near-uniformly fatal in childhood without an allogeneic hematopoietic stem cell transplant. The PDUFA date set by the FDA is March 28, 2026. The Company is eligible for a Rare Pediatric
Disease Priority Review Voucher (PRV), should KRESLADI™ be approved.

A copy of the press release issued in connection with the announcement is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated
into this report by reference.
