Item 8.01.

Other Events.

On February 24, 2021, Halozyme Therapeutics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the pricing of its offering of convertible senior notes due 2027 in an aggregate principal amount of $700 million (the “Notes”). The Company also offered to grant to the initial purchasers of the Notes an option to purchase up to an additional $105 million aggregate principal amount of the Notes. The Notes will be sold to qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
