Item 7.01.

Regulation FD Disclosure.

On February 28, 2022, Roivant Sciences Ltd.’s subsidiary, Genevant Sciences (“Genevant”), issued a press release announcing that Genevant and its licensor, Arbutus Biopharma Corporation, filed a lawsuit in the U.S. District Court for the District of Delaware against Moderna, Inc. and an affiliate (collectively, “Moderna”) seeking damages for infringement of U.S. Patent Nos. 8,058,069, 8,492,359, 8,822,668, 9,364,435, 9,504,651, and 11,141,378 in the manufacture and sale of mRNA-1273, Moderna’s vaccine for COVID-19.

Information in this Item 7.01 to this Current Report on Form 8-K, and in Exhibit 99.1 furnished herewith, shall not be deemed to be “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall such information be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
