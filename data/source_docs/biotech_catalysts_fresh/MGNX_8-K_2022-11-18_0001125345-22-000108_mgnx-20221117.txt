Item 8.01

Other Events

On November 17, 2022, MacroGenics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release in which it announced the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (the “FDA”) has approved the Biologics License Application (“BLA”) for teplizumab, an anti-CD3 monoclonal antibody that was previously developed by the Company and was acquired by Provention Bio, Inc. (“Provention”) in May 2018. Pursuant to the terms of the asset purchase agreement, the approval of teplizmumab triggers a $60 million milestone obligation to the Company from Provention for the FDA approval of teplizumab’s first indication in the United States. A copy of the press release is attached to this Current Report on Form 8-K as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference into this Item 8.01.
