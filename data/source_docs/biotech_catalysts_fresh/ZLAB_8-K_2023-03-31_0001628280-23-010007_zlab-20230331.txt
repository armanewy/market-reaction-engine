Item 8.01 Other Events.

On March 31, 2023, trading in the shares of the Company on The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited was suspended from 9:06 a.m. on March 31, 2023 pending the publication of the Company’s annual results for the year ended December 31, 2022 (the “Annual Results”), which were due to be published on March 31, 2023.
