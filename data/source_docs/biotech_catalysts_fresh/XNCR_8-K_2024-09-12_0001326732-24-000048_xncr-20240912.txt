Item 8.01. Other Events.

On September 12, 2024, Xencor, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the closing of its previously announced underwritten public offering of shares of its common stock, par value $0.01 per share (“Common Stock”). In the offering, the Company sold: (i) an aggregate of 8,093,712 shares of its Common Stock at a public offering price of $18.00 per share, which includes the exercise in full by the underwriters of their option to purchase 1,458,600 additional shares of Common Stock, and (ii) pre-funded warrants to purchase an aggregate of 3,088,888 shares of Common Stock (the “Pre-Funded Warrants”) at a public offering price of $17.99 per share of Common Stock. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
