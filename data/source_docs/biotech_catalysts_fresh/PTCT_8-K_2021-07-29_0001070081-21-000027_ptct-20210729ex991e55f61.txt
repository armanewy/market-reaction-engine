Second Quarter Clinical Updates:

‚óŹThe registration-directed Phase 3 PTC923 phenylketonuria (PKU) trial, APHENITY, is expected to initiate in the third quarter of 2021.

‚óŹPTC has multiple ongoing clinical trials, three of which are registration-directed clinical studies:

o

The MOVE-FA Phase 3 vatiquinone trial for Friedreich ataxia with data anticipated in 2023.

‚óŹ
