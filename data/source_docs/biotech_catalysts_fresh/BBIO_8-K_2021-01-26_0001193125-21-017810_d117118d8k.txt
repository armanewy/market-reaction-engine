Item 1.01.

Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

Purchase Agreement

On January 25, 2021,
BridgeBio Pharma, Inc. (the “Company”) entered into a purchase agreement (the “Purchase Agreement”) with certain financial institutions (collectively the “Initial Purchasers”) pursuant to which the Company agreed to
sell $650,000,000 aggregate principal amount of 2.25% Convertible Senior Notes due 2029 (the “Initial Notes”). The Company also agreed to grant an option (the “Option”) to the Initial Purchasers to purchase all or part of an
additional $97,500,000 aggregate principal amount of 2.25% Convertible Senior Notes due 2029 (the “Additional Notes” and, together with the Initial Notes, the “Notes”).

The Purchase Agreement includes customary representations, warranties and covenants. Under the terms of the Purchase Agreement, the Company has agreed to
indemnify the Initial Purchasers against certain liabilities.

The foregoing description of the Purchase Agreement is qualified in its entirety by
reference to the Purchase Agreement attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
