Item 8.01. Other Events.

On May 16, 2024, Nuvalent, Inc. announced that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration granted breakthrough therapy designation to NVL-655 for the treatment of patients with locally advanced or metastatic anaplastic lymphoma kinase (ALK)-positive non-small cell lung cancer who have been previously treated with two or more ALK tyrosine kinase inhibitors.
