Item 8.01

Other Events

On December 16, 2020, MacroGenics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration has approved the Company’s Biologics License Application for MARGENZATM (margetuximab-cmkb), in combination with chemotherapy, for the treatment of adult patients with metastatic HER2-positive breast cancer who have received two or more prior anti-HER2 regimens, at least one of which was for metastatic disease. A copy of the press release is filed herewith as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and incorporated herein by reference.
