Item 8.01

Other Events.

On December 18, 2023, Mirum Pharmaceuticals, Inc. announced topline results of the Phase 2b EMBARK clinical trial evaluating LIVMARLI® (maralixibat) oral solution in patients with biliary atresia. The study did not meet its primary endpoint or the key secondary endpoints. LIVMARLI was generally well-tolerated with no new safety findings.
