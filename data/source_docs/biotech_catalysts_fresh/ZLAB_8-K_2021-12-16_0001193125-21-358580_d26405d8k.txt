Item 8.01

Other Events.

On December 16, 2021, Zai Lab Limited (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that the China National Medical Products Administration (“NMPA”) has approved its New Drug Application for NUZYRA® (omadacycline) for the treatment of community-acquired bacterial pneumonia and acute bacterial skin and skin structure infections. NUZYRA® was approved as a Category 1 innovative drug by the NMPA and is locally manufactured in China. It is the Company’s fourth product approved over the last 24 months. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
