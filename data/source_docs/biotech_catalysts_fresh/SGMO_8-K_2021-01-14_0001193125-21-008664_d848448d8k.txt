Item 8.01

Other Events.

Sangamo Therapeutics, Inc. is filing this report for the purpose of filing a copy of the slides containing updated follow-up data from the Phase 1/2 Alta study of giroctocogene fitelparvovec (SB-525), an investigational gene therapy for patients with severe hemophilia A, that were presented on December 7, 2020 at the 62nd American Society of Hematology (ASH) Annual Meeting (the “Data Presentation”). A copy of the Data Presentation is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference.
