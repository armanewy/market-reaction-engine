Item 8.01 Other Events.

 

On December 16, 2020, Veracyte, Inc. (the "Company") announced new preliminary performance data for its noninvasive nasal swab test for early lung cancer detection and its Percepta Genomic Atlas for informing treatment decisions at the time of diagnosis. The data were shared during the Company's Virtual Lung Cancer R&D Day, which was held December 16, 2020. A copy of the Company’s press release relating to this announcement is being furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Form 8-K.
