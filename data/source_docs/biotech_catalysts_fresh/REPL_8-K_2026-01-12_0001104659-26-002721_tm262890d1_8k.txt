Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

On January 12, 2026, Replimune Group, Inc. (the “Company”)

of its RP1 and RP2 programs. The Company intends to present information contained in the presentation in meetings with analysts, investors
and others from time to time, including at the 44th Annual J.P. Morgan Healthcare Conference on January 14, 2026. A copy of the presentation
