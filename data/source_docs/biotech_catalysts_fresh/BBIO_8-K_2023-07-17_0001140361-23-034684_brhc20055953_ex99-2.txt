 Acoramidis is a next generation stabilizer that employs multiple strategies to
maximize potency  7  Native TTR circulates in blood as a tetramer  Dissociation into monomers initiates pathogenesis  Monomers aggregate, causing disease  Acoramidis was designed to mimic protective T119M mutation. Acoramidis sees more target
(superior free fraction), binds more target (superior kd2), and glues the target together stronger (enthalpic binding mode).1,2  Disease mechanism  Therapeutic hypothesis  ~130 known destabilizing mutations  ProtectiveT119M mutation  7
