Item 8.01

Other Events.

On September 15, 2024, MacroGenics, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing updated efficacy and safety data related to its Phase 2 TAMARACK clinical trial of vobramitamab duocarmazine in metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer (the “Press Release”). The Company included the updated efficacy and safety data in a poster presentation at the European Society for Medical Oncology (“ESMO”) Congress, taking place in Barcelona, Spain from September 13-17, 2024.

As announced in the Press Release, the Company also hosted an investor conference call on September 16, 2024 in which the Company presented a review of the TAMARACK Phase 2 data and also provided a general corporate update (the “Post-ESMO Conference Call Presentation”).

The full text of the Press Release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein. In addition, a copy of the Post-ESMO Conference Call Presentation is attached as Exhibit 99.2 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.
