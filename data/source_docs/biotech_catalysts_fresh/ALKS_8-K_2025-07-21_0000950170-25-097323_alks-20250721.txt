Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

On July 21, 2025, Alkermes plc (the “Company”) announced positive topline results from Vibrance-1, a phase 2 study evaluating the safety and efficacy of alixorexton (formerly referred to as ALKS 2680) compared to placebo in patients with narcolepsy type 1.

A copy of the press release containing a summary of these topline results is furnished herewith as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference. Detailed safety and efficacy results from the Vibrance-1 study will be shared by the Company at the upcoming World Sleep Congress, taking place September 5-10, 2025.

The information in this Item 7.01, and in Exhibit 99.1 furnished herewith, shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, or incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
